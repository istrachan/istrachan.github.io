About the Photographer

Isabel Strachan is a photographer from Annapolis, Maryland. She currently attends the George Washington University, where she studies Marketing and Art History. 

Isabel is a 2015 YoungArts Winner in Photography, and attended YoungArts Week in Miami in January 2015, where she expanded upon her skills as an artist.

Apart from photography, Isabel is a member of the GW Varsity Sailing Team, and sails competitively around the country.
